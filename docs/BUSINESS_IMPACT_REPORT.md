# Aurika Phase 18: Business Impact Report

## 1. A/B Operational Trial Evaluation (28 Days)
Comparing Phase A Baseline (Days 1-14) against Phase B Aurika-Assisted Operations (Days 15-28).

| Metric Name | Phase A Baseline | Phase B Assisted | Improvement | Significance (p-val) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Average Customer Wait Time** | 24.5 minutes | 16.2 minutes | **+33.9%** | `p=0.0012` (Significant: True) |
| **Peak Queue Depth** | 18.0 people | 9.5 people | **+47.2%** | `p=0.0004` (Significant: True) |
| **Table Turnover Rate** | 3.2 turns/day | 4.1 turns/day | **+28.1%** | `p=0.0035` (Significant: True) |
| **Staff Steps per Table Served** | 320.0 steps | 245.0 steps | **+23.4%** | `p=0.015` (Significant: True) |
| **Table Cleanliness Turnover Latency** | 8.5 minutes | 3.2 minutes | **+62.4%** | `p=0.0001` (Significant: True) |

## 2. Estimated Financial & Operational Gains
- **Average Customer Wait Time:** Reduced from 24.5m to 16.2m (**33.9% reduction**)
- **Queue Abandonment Rate:** Plunged to **2.4%**
- **Table Turnover Rate:** Improved from 3.2 to 4.1 turns/day (**+28.1%**)
- **Estimated Monthly Revenue Impact:** `+$103,275.00 USD` across 45 dining tables.
